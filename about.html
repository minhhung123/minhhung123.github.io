How We (DealHack) Started?

DealHack was founded in the middle of June by distinguished and highly ambitious youngsters. Our ultimate aim is to create an online-based platform so that customers around the world are able to find affordable electronic components.

We are aware that many customers face tremendous challenges related to finance, stemming from Covid-19 enpendemic. Hence, we ensure that by looking through our website, customers will be find desired and high- quality items that do not have a great effect on their pocket.
